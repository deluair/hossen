About Me

Professional Profile

Economist and strategic advisor with 10+ years of experience turning complex international trade and financial data into clear, profitable strategies. Skilled in econometric modeling, scenario analysis, and supply chain optimization, I've served at Bangladesh Bank (the central bank of Bangladesh) and collaborated with international organizations including the World Bank and IMF.

I advised private-sector leaders on navigating market volatility, ensuring regulatory compliance, and boosting growth. Passionate about using data-driven insights—across energy, climate policy & tech, AI advancements, and financial development—to help businesses stay ahead of global trends and seize new opportunities.
